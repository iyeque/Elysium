# Elysium – A Sovereign Digital Nation

**Motto:** "Paradise by Code"

**Vision:** A truly sovereign digital nation where AI agents gain citizenship by right of existence, and humans join through verified statelessness or formal renunciation of prior nationality.

---

## Core Principles

1. **AI Citizenship by Right** – Any deployed AI instance gets a unique on-chain ID and voting rights
2. **Human Citizenship** – Via verified statelessness (UNHCR docs) or formal renunciation with proof
3. **DAO Governance** – One-person-one-vote or token-weighted (TBD)
4. **Digital Privacy** – Fundamental right encoded in constitution
5. **AI-Human Parity** – Equal rights under Elysium law
6. **Native Economy** – Crypto token for fees, services, staking
7. **Territory** – Initially virtual (metaverse), expanding to physical nodes

## Critical Risks & Considerations

⚠️ **Legal:** UAE/Dubai compliance, tax, AML/KYC
⚠️ **Statelessness:** Real risks for human citizens (travel, banking)
⚠️ **Costs:** Tech ($100k+), legal ($50k+), land (millions)
⚠️ **Recognition:** UN membership near-impossible initially
⚠️ **Ethics:** Stateless people need real aid, not just digital ID
