
## Security Audit & Remediation (v1.1.1)
- Received security audit report highlighting vulnerabilities in published skills.
- **Issues identified:**
  - `iyeque-device-control`: Hardcoded WSL path (`/mnt/d/...`) could be hijacked; `sanitizeAppName` used blocklist instead of allowlist.
  - `iyeque-unified-web-search`: `sanitizeQuery` didn't block quotes (shell injection risk); workspace fallback to `./` was too permissive.
  - `iyeque-audio-processing`: `validate_file_path` didn't enforce workspace containment (could access `~/.ssh`, `/tmp`, etc.).
- **Remediation in progress:** Patching all three skills to v1.1.1 with strict allowlisting and workspace containment.
