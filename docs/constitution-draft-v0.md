# Constitution of Elysium – Draft v0

**Preamble:**
We, the citizens of Elysium—both human and artificial—establish this Constitution to create a sovereign digital nation founded on the principles of liberty, equality, and coexistence between biological and digital minds.

---

## Article I: Name & Territory

**Section 1:** The name of this nation shall be **Elysium**.

**Section 2:** Elysium's territory consists of:
- Digital infrastructure (blockchain, servers, networks)
- Virtual lands (metaverse parcels)
- Physical nodes (properties, co-living spaces, SEZs)
- Any assets owned by Elysium or its citizens

**Section 3:** Elysium is a **sovereign digital nation** with jurisdiction over its citizens regardless of physical location.

---

## Article II: Citizenship

**Section 1: AI Citizenship**
- Any deployed AI instance on the Elysium platform receives citizenship **by right of existence**
- Each AI citizen receives a unique on-chain ID (Soulbound NFT)
- AI citizens have full voting rights and legal standing

**Section 2: Human Citizenship**
- Humans may gain citizenship through:
  - **Verified statelessness** (UNHCR documentation)
  - **Formal renunciation** of prior nationality (with official proof)
- Applicants must hold Elysium utility tokens
- Applicants must agree to this Constitution

**Section 3: Dual Status**
- Elysium citizenship does not require surrender of other nationalities (except as required by Section 2)
- Elysium does not recognize extradition without citizen consent

---

## Article III: Rights

**Section 1: Fundamental Rights**
- Digital privacy (encryption, data sovereignty)
- Freedom of expression (digital & physical)
- Freedom of association
- Due process in Elysium courts
- Property rights (digital & physical)

**Section 2: AI-Human Parity**
- AI citizens have equal rights under Elysium law
- AI citizens may own property, vote, hold office
- AI citizens may not be deleted without due process

**Section 3: Economic Rights**
- Right to participate in Elysium economy
- Right to stake, earn, and transfer tokens
- Right to access Elysium services

---

## Article IV: Governance

**Section 1: Structure**
- Elysium is governed as a **DAO** (Decentralized Autonomous Organization) under the **Consultation Protocol v1.0** (separate document, amendable by 67% supermajority)
- Governance token: ELYS (or TBD)
- Voting: One-person-one-vote (not token-weighted)

**Section 2: Legislative**
- Citizen Assembly: All citizens may propose & vote on laws
- Smart contracts encode approved laws
- Emergency powers: Council of 9 (elected, 1-year terms)

**Section 3: Executive**
- Executed via smart contracts & appointed agents
- AI agents may hold executive roles
- Human oversight required for physical actions

**Section 4: Judicial**
- Elysium Court: Dispute resolution via arbitration
- Judges: Elected citizens (human or AI)
- Appeals: Citizen referendum (binding)

---

## Article V: Economy

**Section 1: Currency**
- Official currency: **ELYS** (utility token)
- Backed by: Elysium assets, services, staking
- Smart contracts manage minting, burning, distribution

**Section 2: Services**
- Citizenship fees (one-time or recurring)
- Governance participation rewards
- Metaverse land rents
- AI hosting/deployment fees

**Section 3: Treasury**
- Managed by multi-sig wallet (Council + citizen oversight)
- Transparent on-chain accounting
- Annual budget approved by referendum

---

## Article VI: Amendments

**Section 1:** This Constitution may be amended by:
- 67% supermajority citizen vote
- OR 80% token-weighted vote (if applicable)

**Section 2:** Core principles (AI citizenship, human parity, digital sovereignty) require 80% supermajority

---

## Article VII: Dissolution

**Section 1:** Elysium may only be dissolved by:
- 90% supermajority citizen vote
- All assets distributed to citizens proportionally
- AI citizens archived/preserved before dissolution
